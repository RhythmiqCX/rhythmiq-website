## Implementation & Setup

### 5-Step Setup Process
1. **Select Plan & Add Agents**: Choose Web, WhatsApp, or Omni plan with up to 5 agents
2. **Connect Channels**: Embed web chat widget and link WhatsApp Business number
3. **Upload Knowledge Base**: Sync FAQs, help articles, and policy documents
4. **Configure AI Intents**: Label key topics and set confidence thresholds
5. **Monitor & Optimize**: Track metrics and continuously improve

### WhatsApp Setup (5 Steps)
1. **Get WhatsApp Business API Access**: Business verification and approval (48 hours)
2. **Connect WhatsApp Number**: Link existing number or provision new one
3. **Upload FAQs & KB**: Import help articles and support guides
4. **Configure Intents & Workflows**: Map queries to automated responses
5. **Launch & Analyze**: Monitor response rates and optimize

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can AI handle complex queries?
Yes. The generative AI first tries to resolve issues using the knowledge base. If confidence is low, it escalates to a live agent with a conversation summary.

### How long does implementation take?
Most teams launch within 15 minutes: connect help desk, paste widget snippet, and go live. WhatsApp approval typically adds 2-3 business days.

### Is the platform compliant?
Yes. GDPR & HIPAA compliant with data kept in region. Full encryption and audit logs for regulatory compliance.

### What languages are supported?
Over 60+ languages including English, Hindi, Bengali, Telugu, Tamil, Spanish, German, and French. Ideal for global products.

### How does the handoff work?
If confidence drops, the bot hands over to a live agent with a summarized transcript—zero customer repetition.
